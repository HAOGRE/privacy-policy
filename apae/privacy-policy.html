隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 4 月 22 日

感谢您使用 Apae。本隐私政策说明我们如何（或不如何）处理您的个人信息。

我们不收集任何数据

Apae 不会收集、存储、传输或共享任何用户个人数据。应用的所有功能均在您的设备本地运行，不与任何服务器通信。

设备权限

Apae 使用 Apple 的"屏幕使用时间"（Screen Time）框架来提供屏幕管理功能。相关数据由 Apple 系统在本地管理，开发者无法访问。

第三方服务

Apae 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

儿童隐私

我们不收集任何用户数据，包括儿童数据，符合《儿童在线隐私保护法》（COPPA）的相关要求。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Thank you for using Apae. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle (or do not handle) your personal information.

We Do Not Collect Any Data

Apae does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal user data. All app functionality runs locally on your device and does not communicate with any server.

Device Permissions

Apae uses Apple's Screen Time framework to provide screen management features. All related data is managed locally by the Apple system and is not accessible to the developer.

Third-Party Services

Apae does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

We do not collect any user data, including data from children, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

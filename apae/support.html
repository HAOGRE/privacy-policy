常见问题

为什么 App 无法屏蔽应用？

Apae 需要"屏幕使用时间"权限才能正常工作。请前往设置 → 屏幕使用时间，确认已授权 Apae 使用家庭控制功能。

规则已启用，但应用没有被锁定？

请确认规则已选择目标应用，且当前时间在规则的限制范围内。屏蔽生效可能有约 10 秒的延迟。

如何解除所有限制？

在规则列表中关闭或删除对应规则即可立即解除屏蔽。

忘记了 PIN 码怎么办？

请前往设置 → SOS 救援页面，可重置屏幕使用时间权限以恢复访问。

Apae 会上传我的数据吗？

不会。Apae 完全在本地运行，不连接任何服务器，不收集任何用户数据。

FAQ

Why isn't Apae blocking apps?

Apae requires Screen Time permission to function. Go to Settings → Screen Time and make sure Apae is authorized to use Family Controls.

I enabled a rule but the app isn't blocked?

Make sure the rule has target apps selected and that the current time falls within the rule's restrictions. Blocking may take up to 10 seconds to take effect.

How do I remove all restrictions?

Disable or delete the rule in the rules list to immediately remove the block.

I forgot my PIN. What should I do?

Go to Settings → SOS page to reset Screen Time permission and regain access.

Does Apae upload my data?

No. Apae runs entirely on-device, connects to no servers, and collects no user data.
